HomeAR Application


HomeAR android application is committed to deliver effective solutions to users in visualizing the products under different catalogs. It is developed by an enthusiatic team comprising of four graduate students of MS Software Engineering major. The system uses the feature of Augmented Reality, to help users in decision making. The Augmented reality experience rendered to our users will support them in making better purchase decisions on products they are willing to buy.
